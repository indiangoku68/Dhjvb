Our Accidental Love Story 💍

This is a small website for someone very special to me — HORI. Today marks 4 months of us, and honestly it still feels unreal how everything happened. What started as random conversations slowly turned into something much deeper… And somehow in the middle of everything, we even accidentally took pheras like a married couple. So technically… this is our 4-month anniversary story. ❤️

First Time We Met

Not exactly the first time we met, because technically we had already seen each other before. But the moment that really stayed in my mind was when the Hindi students of Section C were moving to Section B and Section A. And there you were… sitting quietly in the corner of the room. I’ve said this so many times already — but seriously, you looked so composed and smart. I remember feeling a little intimidated, like "lol is this class even for me? Everyone looks smart af." And honestly, at that moment you seemed as distant to me as any other girl in that classroom. In my eyes, you were just this very composed, calm person sitting there.

Closeness

But things started changing because of something completely random. My sudden friendship with Topi ended up making me meet you more often. We talked a few times during the student assembly, and especially during her sister’s birthday. And at that point something felt different. You actually seemed really comforting to me. After that birthday we ended up exchanging Instagram IDs, and that’s when everything really started. We began talking more often… then more frequently… and before we even realized it, we were talking constantly till late nights. It was a really fast friendship. And honestly, when I think about it now… our friendship had already become so close that it was basically like a relationship in its final stage, just without the label.

When Things Started Feeling Different

I think this really started during the farewell. That night we suddenly got super close, really fast. And honestly I can proudly say that I had never been that physically comfortable around a girl before in my whole life like I was with you. That was the first time I lent my coat to someone, the first time I brushed someone’s hair (by my own will for God’s sake), and none of it felt awkward. From that day onwards I felt really close and comfortable around you. Being around you started to feel like a second home. Then came another moment — the summer camp. And honestly, I think by that time I had already started keeping you at the center of my life. Because I wasn’t even planning to go to the camp initially, but you convinced me. Things became really real on 6th November. That day holds a core feeling I don’t think I’ll ever forget.

The Mandir Incident

You remember our visit to the mandir on 27th February, right? That normal little visit somehow turned into something much funnier and much more special. Because somehow… we ended up taking 4 pheras around the mandir. And the funny part is that in Assamese tradition, 4 pheras basically mean marriage. So if we follow that logic… we accidentally got married that day. Just pause for a second and think about that memory… before moving on to the next page. ❤️

The 4 Pheras We Took 💍

That day at the mandir, taking 4 pheras unknowingly completed something very meaningful. In Assamese tradition, 4 pheras already mean marriage. Which means… without planning it… without realizing it… we accidentally got married.

Four Months of Us

Four months. When someone hears the number, it might sound small. But when I actually think about everything that happened between us, it feels like we lived through countless moments, emotions, and memories. Like our first hug near the library storage room, first kiss at the staircase outside the closed shops, the glances, smiles, arguments, disagreements, and broken expectations — yet we survived. Every memory together, big or small, has formed the beautiful chaos of us. And somehow… through everything, you became my comfort, my home, and my accidental wife. ❤️

But these four months proved that what we share is strong enough to survive anything — even miles apart. I imagine us keeping close through messages, calls, and small surprises. I imagine our reunion after months apart, hugging endlessly, laughing at memories, sharing everything we missed, and realizing that distance never diminished what we have. Through misunderstandings, waiting, and loneliness, our love will grow stronger, because even now… we are already married in spirit and heart. ❤️

HORI, these four months have been incredible, chaotic, and beautiful. From noticing you in that corner of the classroom, to late-night talks, first hugs, first kisses, silly arguments, comforting moments, and our accidental pheras, every second with you has been unforgettable. Even if LDR stretches for months, our hearts are already together. Thank you for being my accidental wife, my comfort, and my best friend. I love you forever ❤️
